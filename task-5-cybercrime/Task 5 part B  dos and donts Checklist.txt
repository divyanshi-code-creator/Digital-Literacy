Part B – Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
Do’s
Think before you post, consider long-term impact.
Use respectful and polite language.
Protect your privacy (adjust account settings).
Verify information before sharing.
Maintain a positive digital footprint.
Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly.
Don’t engage in online arguments or hate speech.
Don’t post misleading or fake content.
Don’t overshare academic or confidential details.
Don’t ignore digital etiquette or platform rules.
